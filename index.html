Type Type Revenge is a typing practice game modeled after the classic iPhone game, Tap Tap Revenge.Press p to play!

HOW TO PLAY

When the falling letters match up with their target outlines at the bottom of the screen, hit the corresponding letter on your keyboard to earn points.

The targets will glow when you correctly type the letters,
and will turn red if you don't.

Maintain a streak of correctly pressed notes to earn more points!

Make sure you turn your volume up so you can hear the music.
Good luck!
